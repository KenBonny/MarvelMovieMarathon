Welcome to the Marvel Movie Marathon

This site is to help you detemine in what order you can best view the Marvel movies and series so you can watch them in a chronological order. No more wondering where this item came from or how this guy got his powers, while it will be explained in a film or episode that was produced at a later time, but which you should've watched first.

For clarification, I'm not affiliated with Marvel and I'm doing this in part for myself, so I know the order in which to watch the films/series again later. A very BIG thanks to them for making such awesome films and series!
If I'm in violation of certain copyright laws, contact me and we'll work something out. You can also contact me if I overlooked something or I should add a movie or series.

A big thanks to The Internet Movie DataBase for providing such an awesome service!
